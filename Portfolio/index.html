MY PROJECTS :)

(A)SMART BLIND STICK

Smart Blind Stick is a device designed to help guide the visually impaired by detecting objects (Using Aurduino UNO and Ultra-sonic Sensors)and portray the information to them(Using Buzzer and Motor). This reduces the human effort and gives better understanding of the surrounding.Furthermore it also provides an opportunity for visually impaired people to move from one place to another without being assisted by others. The device can also be used in old age homes where old age people have difficulty in their day to day activities due to decreased vision.

(B)SMART POWER STRIP

The Smart Power Strip revolutionizes all common power strips. Users have the ability to remotely toggle the individual outlets on the Smart Power Strip from a smart phone app and can also monitor how much energy the devices connected to the Smart Power Strip consume.
